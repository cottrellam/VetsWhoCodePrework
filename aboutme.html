Bio

I was born and raised in a small town in northcentral Indiana. In my early years we lived on a small hobby farm. We had a couple of cows, some geese, chickens, and rabbits. My first car was a Dodge Dart Swinger. I was on the dive team in high school. I love riding my skateboard all over town. My first computer was a Commodore Vic-20. I used that to learn basic when I was about 11-12.

When I turned 18 I joined the Navy and became an electronics tech. I was stationed on the USS Orion in Sardignia Italy.

When I got out of the Navy I stayed around in Norfolk, Virginia where I met my future and current spouse. We moved from Virginia to Indiana where I started working as tech support for a local internet provider. This is where I first started learning html. After that we moved to San Diego, California where I worked in the health insurance industry. Then we moved to Indianapolis where I learned PHP and developed web applications using the LAMP stack.

I then got a job as a software developer at Regis Corporation in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This is where I developed my my coding skills with C# and Angular.
